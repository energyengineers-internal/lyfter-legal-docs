2. Types of information we collect

a. Information you provide directly

  • Account registration: name, phone number, email address, profile photo, biography/description.
  • Vehicle and ride details: vehicle type, registration number (if disclosed), route, price or per‑km charge, availability, pickup/drop locations.
  • Messages and communications: in‑app messages, booking requests, reports, feedback, and ratings.
  • Support and reports: content you submit when reporting incidents or contacting support.
  • Optional data: any additional information you enter into your profile or listings.

b. Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data: device identifiers, operating system, app version, IP address, crash logs, analytics (how you use the Platform).
  • Location data: approximate or trip-related location data you provide or that you permit the app to collect (only if you enable location features). Currently Lyfter’s core features do not require continuous background location; if we change this, we will update this Policy and obtain any required permissions and consent.

c. Information from third parties

  • If you link or sign in via third‑party services (e.g., Google, Facebook), we may receive profile information from those providers per their permissions and APIs.
  • Publicly available information or data from partners (if any).

3. How we use your information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the Platform (create accounts, publish listings, process booking requests, deliver messages).
  • Facilitate connections between Riders and Passengers and display relevant ride information.
  • Communicate with you about bookings, confirmations, safety notices, updates, or policy changes.
  • Investigate reports, enforce Terms, prevent fraud, and respond to legal requests.
  • Improve and personalize the Platform (analytics, product development, performance monitoring).
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect Lyfter’s rights, property, and users’ safety.

4. How we share information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

a. With other Users

Information you post in ride listings or profile fields (e.g., vehicle type, route, contact details when provided) will be visible to matched Users, prospective passengers/riders, and participants in the relevant Ride.

b. With service providers

Third‑party vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, messaging, payment processors if/when added, legal or compliance services). These providers may process data only as instructed by Lyfter and under contractual obligations.

c. For legal reasons and safety

  • When required by law, court order, or government request; to respond to claims or legal process; or to enforce our Terms.
  • To protect the safety, rights, property, or security of Lyfter, our users, or the public (for example, in response to reports of illegal or dangerous conduct).

d. Business transfers

If Lyfter merges, is acquired, or sells assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users where required by law.

e. Aggregated or anonymized data

We may share anonymized, de-identified, or aggregated data that does not reasonably identify you.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. Payments and financial data

Currently, payments are handled offline (cash) between Users. Lyfter does not process payments, store payment card data, or collect transaction histories.

If we introduce in‑app payments, we will publish an updated policy describing payment processing, data handling, and any use of payment service providers.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction (encryption in transit, access controls, secure hosting).

No system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. If a data breach affecting your personal data occurs, we will comply with applicable law regarding notice and mitigation.

7. Data retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide the Platform, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce Agreements, and fulfill legitimate business purposes.

When data is no longer needed, we delete, anonymize, or aggregate it consistent with our data retention policies and legal obligations.
