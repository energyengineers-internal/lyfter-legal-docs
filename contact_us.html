Lyfter Support

Contact Us

Need help or have a question? You can reach us directly from inside the Lyfter mobile app.

How to contact us from the app

The fastest way to reach Lyfter Support is through the Contact Us section in the app.

  1. Open the Lyfter mobile app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Select Contact Us.
  4. Send us a message with details (issue description, phone number used to sign in, and any screenshots if available).

After you send a message, our team will review it and get back to you within 4–5 business days.

Account deletion request

You can also request account deletion from the same place:

  • Open Profile → Contact Us.
  • Send a message stating that you want to delete your account.
  • Include the phone number (or account identifier) associated with your account.

For security reasons, we may ask for additional information to verify ownership before processing deletion.

What to include in your message

  • Your registered phone number
  • A short description of the issue or request
  • Time and date the issue occurred (if applicable)
  • Any screenshots or error messages

Response time

We typically respond within 4–5 business days after receiving your message.

Response times may be longer during holidays or peak support volume.
